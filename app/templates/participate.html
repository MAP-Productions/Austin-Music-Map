Tag them as you take them!

  1. Tag #AustinMusicMap and the #Location
  2. Geolocate the media or tag the zipcode (e.g. #78702)
  3. Add descriptive tags (e.g. #NeonSign or #Taxidermy) to help build the playlists!

Flickr

  1. Login or create an account (it’s quick and free)
  2. Join the Austin Music Map Group
  3. Upload your photographs
  4. Tag your photos with austinmusicmap. This step is really important. If you don’t tag your photos properly, we will not be able to display them on our site.
  5. Add descriptive tags (e.g. #NeonSign or #Taxidermy) to help build the playlists!

Instagram

  1. Download the instagram app on your phone
  2. Create your account and be sure your account is set to "public" (not "private user")
  3. Tag your instagrams with #austinmusicmap. This step is really important. If you don’t tag your instagrams properly, we will not be able to display them on our site.
  4. Add descriptive tags (e.g. #NeonSign or #Taxidermy) to help build the playlists!

SoundCloud

  1. Login or create an account (it’s quick and free)
  2. Upload your audio
  3. Change the license to Creative Commons
  4. Tag your audio with austinmusicmap. This step is really important. If you don’t tag your audio properly, we will not be able to display them on our site.
  5. Add descriptive tags (e.g. #NeonSign or #Taxidermy) to help build the playlists!

YouTube

  1. Login or create an account (it’s quick and free)
  2. Upload your videos
  3. Tag your videos with austinmusicmap. This step is really important. If you don’t tag your photos properly, we will not be able to display them on our site.
  4. Add descriptive tags (e.g. #NeonSign or #Taxidermy) to help build the playlists!
